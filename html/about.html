Let me tell you about me.

Hello hello! My name is Nika Faith Ablao and I'm a UX Designer & Researcher based in sunny San Diego, California. I'm currently a senior at Cornell University in Ithaca, New York and am majoring in Information Science with a triple concentration in UX, Design Culture & Production, and Data Science and am minoring in Business.

I believe that we must use design to reflect how we view the world. By combining this with a thorough understanding of our audience, we can create beautiful, yet straightforward interfaces that afford enjoyable user experiences. I'm a designer because I see technology as a tool which we can use to simplify and augment our lives. However, this is only achieved with properly designed interfaces — and I want to make them.

When I'm not looking for the perfect font, you can find me doodling in my bullet journal, coloring too many thigs pink, practicing hand lettering, playing rugby, or struggling to sing Broadway.
